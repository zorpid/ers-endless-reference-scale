Same symbol in green is used for www.endlessgreen.se saving the planet. In blue we use it for www.endlesssolution.se where we develop-sell-produce systems and sensors for better environment.

The shop is non-profit to get money for EndlessGreenPrize. It is built on the 4 elements Air-water-soil-fire.

Endless Reference Scale is a tendency scale to get “Early Warning” and collect data over time. Similar to a traffic light. It is used for our sensors connected to the cloud.

One good example is our GWS GroundWaterSensor connected to the cloud and cellphone. The intention is to build up a database that can be used for research or tendency warning. There is one million drilled wells only in Sweden.

For Air we have CO2 and pollution sensors. For agriculture we will have different Tree- and soil-sensors.
